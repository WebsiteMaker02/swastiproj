Our Services

Currently ArogyaDeepa has covered over 15,000 people through it’s registered Wellness Clinics located in Bommanahalli & Kamakshipalya, Bangalore with 25+ staffing capacity.

2. Financial Inclusion / Security

3. Social Protection & Entitlements

4. Safety, Security and Justice

5. Facilitate self and wage employment to improve the livelihoods
